Simulator Core Assumptions

  • 1-Year Lock-in: All fixed deposits run for exactly 12 months.
  • Shifted Availability: Base capital additions are shifted forward by 1 month to set the start date (placed on the 1st).
  • No Withdrawals: Assumes zero cash withdrawals out of the capital.
  • Maturity Payouts: Interest earned is generated and paid out only on maturity (end of 1 year).
  • Auto Rollover: On maturity, matured principal and earned interest are immediately rolled into another 1-year FD at the new rate.
  • No Minimum sum limits: No minimum balance restrictions apply to opening FDs.
